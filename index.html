📖 About us

Triumph Fabulous Divine
International School

Triumph Fabulous Divine International School is a co-educational School that caters to children between 1-12 years. The school is located in Emene, Enugu state, Nigeria.

She trains children to be morally and academically sound and bold at public speaking. Triumph Fabulous Divine International school is blessed with skilful and trained teachers who bring out the best in the pupils.
